Hi there Soren,

Thanks for your interest.  I know our mailing lists are a bit empty,
so here is what I know has been said:
* We aren't going to use mogredotnet
* Many people want ogredotnet, and some are trying to revive the
project, some like me want to create a higher level graphics engine
above ogredotnet that we can swigify for openviewer specifically.
* Python is pretty far out of the capabilities of most people in this
project.  I think many of us assume LibSecondLife (C#) is going to be
the primary thing we are building on
* Our repository is horribly broken and often.

My suggestion to you if your interested in some of the popular ideas
going around:
* Learn how to use swig
* Learn how to use GTK#
* Take a look at the Sleek project and see how it uses LibSecondLife

- Daedius
