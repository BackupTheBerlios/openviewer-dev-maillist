>
> Example:
> Your in Deepgrid on fantasy island  at coordinates 123.452.205 and you
> render a new door.  The door automatically configures itself for the
> appropriate information (Same as a landmark today but with additional server
> information and settings).  A copy of the door is stored in a local database
> on your PC as well as in world.
>
> Later that day meet someone in  OSGrid tell them about your place and render
> the door you made.  They walk through the door and are in OSgrid at your
> house. (Check out croquet)
>
> I fully agree we absolutely need to be able to configure and customize
> settings (click on door up pops an in world control panel and enter all the
> settings)  but hopefully that's rare as it's so much easier from the user
> perspective if they simply walk through a pre configured door.
>
> In terms of all the other configurations could this all be made just as
> intuitive using an in world 3D control panel with a bunch of prims on it.
> Click on the phone prim to configure voice, click on sun prim to configure
> sky click on a voice prim to chat, camera prim for camera etc etc.   This
> way even my Mother could use it and if she needed help she could render up
> her control panel give me access then watch as I show her how to do
> something.
>
> Anyway just food for thought.
