I have been chatting on SLeek Discussion boards with Paul Clement (the
project owner).  I talked a bit with him about the vision of
OpenViewer and our pondering of a GTK# port of SLeek.  He seemed
really interested on making SLeek compatible as a UI module, I invited
him to come by our IRC chat rooms and talk.  I'll repost when I get
more follow up information on a specific date and time.

As mentioned before.  I believe the project offers us a group real
world implementation of the LibSL library, and can help us start a
healthy direction for the foundations of OpenViewer model side.  We
will need to think about a few things:

-  How do we make our platform easily accessible for
creators/developers/maintainers of OpenViewer UIs ( documentation,
APIs, etc).
- What features are we going to need to be successful model side.
Ability to login to multiple grids? Abstraction of configuration  (
not everyone may want to control openviewer by our xml files )
- How can we provide intuitive interfaces between openviewer and the
UI for the various types o f UIs that will come to exist (3D, 2D,
Winforms, Console)?

Let these thoughts stew a bit in your smart creative minds out there =)
